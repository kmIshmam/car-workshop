1.	Introduction

The aim of this is to create an application that is helpful while maintaining the records and transactions. In the existing system, it is quite difficult to maintain the all kinds of information individually and to supply for the customers who are eagerly waiting to come up with their demands. Customer has to face difficulty in order to know the information of proposed services, like repairing, modifying, car paint, and other information in a domain. Our main idea is to develop a system where we can have all the required information for a user customer in order to effectively make him or her interested in the process of delivering our service. 

In this proposed system, this application can maintain information and details of user’s car like vehicle type(sedan, SUV,etc), vehicle brand (Toyota, Nissan, Mitsubishi, Audi, Mercedes, BMW) fuel type (Diesel, Octane), service point, Registration number, customer phone number, date (direct Calendar)  and other information. We can also view the details of a car which are kept for services effectively and we can search our desired garage and ask for booking and finally create invoice. With this, customer can get the required information quickly.   

Now, we will use a case story to show how it will work:

Mahmud is a customer who wants to repair, paint and modify his car. Instead of going to the garage, he can easily view all the information of the garage shop provides; repairing, car painting, modifying parts and so on. Searching and selecting his desired services, he will be asked to fill up his form. After all of that, and invoice will be created.

After all, the records and transactions will be stored in the database.
